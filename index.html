How NeatNest Works

From registration to scheduled pickup in just a few simple steps.

1

Register Your Home

Sign up with your mobile number and address. Admin reviews and activates your account.

2

Admin Assigns Schedule

Admin assigns your pickup dates and times. You'll see them highlighted on your calendar.

3

Pay Monthly Bill

4

Get Notified

Receive a push notification the moment the driver arrives at your gate — never miss a pickup.
